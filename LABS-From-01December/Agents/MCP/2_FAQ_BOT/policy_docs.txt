
Company Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to various leave options such as annual leave, sick leave, maternity leave, and paternity leave.
To apply for paternity leave, submit the form via the HR portal at https://intranet.hr/paternity-leave.
You are eligible for up to 15 days of paid paternity leave.

IT Support Ticket:
To report a technical issue, please visit https://intranet.support/ticket and fill out the form.
Make sure to include a description of the issue, screenshots if possible, and contact details.

Reimbursement Policy:
All reimbursement claims must be submitted by the 10th of the following month.
Use the reimbursement form available at https://intranet.hr/reimbursements.
